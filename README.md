## Parent Portal Dashboard

Project Context
Families navigating child care support programs often need to manage information across multiple providers, agencies, schedules, documents, payments, eligibility requirements, and communications. Important actions are frequently distributed across emails, letters, portals, and support channels, making it difficult for parents to understand what requires attention and what actions need to be completed next.
This proof of concept explores how a single, personalized dashboard experience could help parents stay informed, organized, and proactive by bringing the most relevant information into one place.

## Persona
Daniel OrtizParent of two children enrolled in multiple child care and enrichment programs.
Daniel regularly manages attendance records, payment schedules, eligibility renewals, required documentation, provider communications, and program updates. His primary need is not access to more information, but clarity around what matters most right now and what actions require attention.

⸻

## Proof of Concept

This prototype demonstrates a single-page dashboard designed to provide parents with a personalized snapshot of their child care ecosystem.
The experience combines family information, program enrollment details, upcoming schedules, required actions, important documents, and AI-generated recommendations into a unified view. Rather than requiring parents to navigate multiple systems, the dashboard surfaces relevant information, highlights potential issues, and helps users take action directly from the home experience.
The concept explores how AI can move beyond answering questions and instead proactively identify opportunities, reminders, missing documentation, upcoming deadlines, and cost-saving recommendations.

⸻

What Works in this Prototype
The current proof of concept demonstrates:
	●	Personalized family overview and household context
	●	Child care program enrollment and progress tracking
	●	Payment and attendance management workflows
	●	Unified schedule and key dates experience
	●	Action Center for notifications, tasks, and document requests
	●	AI-generated recommendations and reminders
	●	Contextual actions surfaced directly within the dashboard
	●	Visual indicators for upcoming events and important milestones
This prototype focuses on information architecture, content hierarchy, prioritization, and interaction design rather than backend integrations.
